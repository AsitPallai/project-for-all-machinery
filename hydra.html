ACE FX 230 — Key Technical Description

Concise overview of engine, hydraulic, rope/hoist, brake/air systems, structure, and service schedule.

1. Engine

  • Powered by a Volvo Eicher E483 CRDi diesel engine.
  • 4-cylinder, water-cooled, turbocharged engine with an electronic control unit (ECU).
  • Rated power: 101 HP (74 kW) at 2200 RPM.

2. Hydraulic System

  • Hydraulic winch system — the hoist is driven by a hydraulic motor.
  • 3-part slotted telescopic boom, extended hydraulically and synchronously.
  • Full-power “orbittrol” steering using twin double-acting hydraulic cylinders; articulation angle ~ 40° on each side.
  • Gradeability (ability to climb): up to 40%.

3. Rope / Hoist System

  • Rope diameter: 13 mm.
  • Hoist speed (single-line): 35 m/min (line speed).
  • Winch is 6-fall (six parts/falls of rope) for mechanical advantage.
  • Self-adjusting hook block plus rope tensioner to maintain rope tension and alignment.

4. Brake / Air System

  • Service brakes (movement): fully air-actuated on both front and rear wheels.
  • Parking brake: pneumatically operated, fail-safe type.
  • Safety features: audio over-hoist warning and hose-failure protection.

5. Structure / Dimensions

  • Maximum lifting capacity: 23 tonnes.
  • Maximum boom height: approximately 18.3 m.
  • Wheelbase: 5100 mm.
  • Turning radius (outer edge): about 8.2 m.
  • Tyres: Front = 12.00 × 24 – 20 PR (4 nos), Rear = 14.00 × 25 – 20

6. Safety / Misc Features

  • Low center-of-gravity design for better stability.
  • Self-adjusting hook block for load alignment.
  • Load-Moment Indicator (LMI) / Safe-Load Indicator to prevent overloading.
  • Standard cab with ROPS and rotomould canopy for safety and visibility.
  • Electrical: 12 V, 100 Ah negative-earth battery.

Summary

  • The ACE FX 230 is a 23‑ton hydraulic pick-and-carry crane, designed for lifting and transporting loads on-site.
  • Powered by a robust 101 HP Volvo-Eicher engine suitable for construction and material handling tasks.
  • The hydraulic system provides boom extension, steering, and winch operation via hydraulic motors.
  • Rope/hoist setup (6-fall, 13 mm rope, self-adjusting hook) supports reliable, controlled lifting.
  • Air-operated service brakes and a fail-safe parking brake provide safe movement control.
  • Design- and system-level safety (low CG, LMI, hose-failure protection) enhance operational safety.
