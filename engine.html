Thermostart

  • Thermo-start is used to heat the intake air of a diesel engine to improve cold starting and combustion.

Why it is used:

  • Preheats intake air: Thermo-start heats the air in the intake manifold so the air entering the cylinders is warm.
  • Improves cold starting:Cold diesel engines struggle to ignite fuel; warm air helps proper combustion.
  • Reduces white smoke & misfiring:Better combustion during start-up lowers unburnt fuel and smoke.
  • Protects engine & battery:Faster starting means less cranking time, reducing load on the starter motor and battery.
  • Simple & reliable system:Uses diesel fuel and an electric heating element—commonly used in heavy equipment and older diesel engines

The most common reason for Diesel Engine over-hauling

1.What are the symptoms that indicate engine overhauling is required?

  • Low power
  • Hard starting
  • Excessive smoke
  • High oil consumption
  • Abnormal noise
  • Low compression / low oil pressure

2.Which engine parts are normally replaced during overhauling?

  • Piston, piston rings
  • Cylinder liner
  • Main & connecting rod bearings
  • Valve guides, valve seats
  • Gaskets & oil seals

3.What causes excessive wear in heavy equipment engines?

  • Continuous heavy load operation
  • Poor lubrication
  • Overheating
  • Dust contamination
  • Poor fuel quality
  • Delayed maintenance

4.How do you check whether overhauling is needed?

  • Compression test
  • Oil consumption check
  • Blow-by inspection
  • Oil pressure measurement
  • Smoke analysis

5.What happens if overhauling is delayed?

  • Severe internal damage
  • Engine seizure
  • Crankshaft damage
  • High repair cost
  • Sudden machine breakdown
